Red Curry

Description

Taken from Allrecipes (submitted by Emma Maher):

This red lentil curry is rich and hearty. It's great as a main meal rather than a

side dish like the more traditional Indian dhal. Don't let the ingredient list faze

you â€” this is an easy dish to make. This dish is excellent served with basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups red lentils
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 tablespoons curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teapsoon ground tumeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger
  • 1 can tomato puree

Steps

  1. Wash lentils in cold water until water runs clear.
  2. Put lentils in a pot. Bring to a boil then cover and simmer.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet. Cook and stir onions in hot oil.
  4. Mix rest of ingredients togehter in a large bowl.
  5. Stir in tomato puree and lentils.
